Smile More

This website was developed to help cheer up children in difficult situations because of a disablity
or cripiling illness at the Hack Manchester event in 2017. The website is based around other people
cheerin up those who struggle go out with friends and have a social life. The site alows people to send messages
and links to people in the form of "gifts". The recipient can then reply and/or start a conversation
with this person.

We hope this website will help get people connected with others in a similar situation or give them something
to look forward to.It also helps them make friends and talk to people. This is a very basic version of the
website and we hope to add extra features.
